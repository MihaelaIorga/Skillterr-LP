What is Skillterr?

A place where young professionals can practice and hone their skills while helping other members with theirs, seek professional help and advice and connect with like-minded people.

Swap Skills

Help other members that need your skills and in exchange, whenever you will need someone’s help, you just have to ask.

Network

Get to know and work with people who have complementary skills, more experience or simply some fresh ideas. It’s a win-win.

Grow

Become an expert at what you already know by practicing and learn new skills with help from your network.
